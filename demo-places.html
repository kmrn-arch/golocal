📍 Top Places in Baku

Must-see attractions for every traveler.

Old City (İçərişəhər)

UNESCO World Heritage Site. Walled medieval city in the heart of Baku. Free to wander, museums charge entry.

Flame Towers

Iconic 3-tower complex visible across the city. Best at night — full LED light show every 30 min.

Maiden Tower (Qız Qalası)

12th-century tower with panoramic views. Entry 15 AZN. Located inside Old City.

Heydar Aliyev Center

Zaha Hadid masterpiece. Stunning fluid architecture. Entry around 15 AZN.

Baku Boulevard

Caspian seafront promenade. Free to walk, perfect for sunset. Cafes & rides along the way.

Yanar Dağ (Burning Mountain)

Natural gas fire that burns continuously. 25 km from city, day-trip favorite.
