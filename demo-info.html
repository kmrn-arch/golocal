ℹ️ Tourist Information — Baku

Everything a first-time visitor should know.

💱

Currency

Azerbaijani Manat (AZN). 1 USD ≈ 1.7 AZN. Cards widely accepted in city.

🔌

Electricity

220V, Type C / F plugs (European standard).

🗣️

Languages

Azerbaijani official. Russian widely spoken. English in tourist areas.

🕐

Time Zone

UTC+4 (AZT). No daylight savings.

📅

Best Time to Visit

April–June and September–October. Mild weather, fewer crowds.

🛂

Visa

e-Visa available for 90+ countries via evisa.gov.az — usually issued in 3 days.
