Our Mission

Making travel safer for everyone

GoLocal exists to remove the stress from tourism — through trust, transparency, and technology.

Our story

GoLocal was born from a simple observation: travelers everywhere face the same problems — language barriers, safety concerns, hidden prices, and unreliable information. We are building the app we wish we had on every trip.

Where we are going

We are starting in Baku, Azerbaijan — a beautiful, growing tourist destination. From there, we will expand to other cities, then countries, and ultimately become a global travel companion.

🇦🇿

Phase 1: Baku

Launch and perfect the experience in our home city.

🌍

Phase 2: Region

Expand across Azerbaijan, Georgia, Turkey, and the Caspian region.

🚀

Phase 3: Global

Bring GoLocal to travelers worldwide.
